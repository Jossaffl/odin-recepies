Chorizo & mozzarella gnocchi bake

Cosy mildly spicy chorizo gnocchi baked over with mozzarella is an amazing way to get dinner on the table.

Soft and fluffy gnocchi snuggled into a rich tomato and grilled pepper saucy with crispy bits of chorizo,

all baked under a blanket of bubbling golden mozzarella!

Oh, My Days!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 120g chorizo, diced
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 600g fresh gnocchi
  • 125g mozzarella ball, cut into chunks
  • small bunch of basil, torn
  • green salad, to serve

Steps

First Stage

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat.
  2. ry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft.
  3. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more.
  4. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, and season.
  5. Bring to a simmer, then add the gnocchi and cook for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft.
  6. Heat the grill to high.

Second stage

  1. Stir Â¾ of the mozzarella and most of the basil through the gnocchi.
  2. Divide the mixture between six ovenproof ramekins, or put in one baking dish.
  3. Top with the remaining mozzarella, then grill for 3 mins, or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  4. Season, scatter over the remaining basil and serve with green salad.
